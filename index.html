Echoes of the Monuments

A Spomenik-Inspired Puzzle and Exploration Game

About

Echoes of the Monuments is a character-driven exploration game that blends historical mystery and surreal environments inspired by the Spomeniks of the former Yugoslavia. You play as a group of Balkan teens unraveling puzzles tied to monuments lost in time.

Characters

Maja – The brave older sister, strong and dependable.
Iskra – Keeps a lighter and a cool head. Mysterious.
Lucija – Great at decoding symbols. Clever and calm.
Vuk – The smallest but sneaky and resourceful. Uses a toy to punch things.
